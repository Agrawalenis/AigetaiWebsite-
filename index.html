Where do I download the new Dream Machine app or access the website? +

You can download the new Dream Machine app on iOS from the App Store. Simply search for "Dream Machine".

Note: Android version is not available.

Web access: dream-machine.lumalabs.ai

What happened to my old Dream Machine account, payment plan, & generations? +

Your old Dream Machine account, plan, and generations are fully migrated.

  • Generations: Now under "Ideas" in app/web.
  • Plans: Converted to credit-based system.
  • All content remains accessible with updated tools.

Can I still use my existing credits and plans? +

Yes! All your previously purchased credits and plans are still valid and available in the new system. They will automatically appear in your account balance once you log in.

Is Dream Machine available for Android users? +

Currently, Dream Machine is only available on iOS and Web. An Android release is planned for the future, but no official launch date is confirmed yet.

How do I contact support if I face issues? +

You can contact our support team by:

  • Using the "Help" section inside the app.
  • Emailing us at support@dreammachine.ai
  • Visiting our official support page.

Do I need to create a new account or can I use my old login? +

No need to create a new account! You can use your existing login credentials to access the new Dream Machine app or website.
